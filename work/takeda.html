Case study — Key Visual

Takeda

From Japan, now in Egypt.

ClientTakeda
SectorPharma / Healthcare
RoleArt Director
DisciplineKey Visual · Art Direction
01 — The challenge

Announce an arrival, not an ad.

Takeda was opening its office in Egypt. The visual had to mark a Japanese healthcare brand planting roots in a new home — with respect for both cultures.

02 — The idea

Bridge two worlds in one frame.

Japanese heritage — the torii gate, the stillness — meets an Egyptian welcome, signalling a brand crossing from one country into another.

03 — Craft

Calm, cinematic, considered.

Warm light and a serene composition give it the gravity of a milestone, not the noise of a promotion.
