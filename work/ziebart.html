Case study — Key Visual

Ziebart

Protect what moves you.

ClientZiebart
SectorAutomotive / Protection
RoleArt Director
DisciplineKey Visual · Art Direction
01 — The challenge

Make protection desirable.

Ziebart makes protection films for cars — drive through dust, storms and weather without a scratch. The visual had to make 'protection' feel premium, not technical.

02 — The idea

Shield the thing that moves you.

The car stands untouched against the elements — proof that whatever the conditions, the finish stays flawless.

03 — Craft

Reflections as proof.

Studio-grade light, deep contrast and a hero-object treatment make the flawless surface the entire argument.
