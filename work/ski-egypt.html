Case study — Key Visual

Ski
Egypt

Ski with Sultan.

ClientSki Egypt
SectorLeisure / Attraction
RoleArt Director
DisciplineKey Visual · Art Direction
01 — The challenge

Sell snow in the desert — with personality.

Ski Egypt is a novelty; it needed a face. The visual had to make the experience feel fun and personal, not just 'there's snow.'

02 — The idea

Meet Sultan, the star of the slope.

The park's famous penguin becomes your companion — come ski, and slide down the snow with Sultan himself.

03 — Craft

Cold light, warm fun.

Crisp winter grading and dynamic action keep the energy high while the character makes it memorable.
