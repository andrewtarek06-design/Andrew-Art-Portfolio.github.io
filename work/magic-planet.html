Magic
Planet

Age is just a number.

ClientMagic Planet
SectorFamily Entertainment
RoleArt Director
DisciplineKey Visual · Art Direction
01 — The challenge

Break the 'kids only' box.

Family entertainment centres are seen as kids' turf. The visual had to say the fun belongs to everyone — with no age limit.

02 — The idea

Let grandma take the controls.

The hero moment: an older woman completely lost in a game, proving the line on the wall — age is just a number — the second you walk in.

03 — Craft

Joy in neon.

Electric colour, motion and arcade glow build pure energy, with the character owning the frame so the message reads instantly.
