World Cup

A cinematic hype film for the season — built with AI.

ClientMajid Al Futtaim
RoleArt Director · AI Artist
DisciplineAI Film · Motion
Runtime0:60
01 — The challenge

Big-screen energy, on a small timeline.

Tie mall energy to World Cup fever with a film that feels like a real ad — on a schedule and budget traditional production couldn't hit.

02 — The idea

Direct it like a film. Generate it like AI.

The thinking stays human — concept, shot list and art direction first. AI builds the imagery and the world; motion and edit give it rhythm. Speed without losing the eye.

03 — The pipeline

From prompt to picture-lock.

Concept & shot list → AI-generated frames and sequences directed to one consistent look → motion, grade and sound to cut it into a single film. Consistency was the hard part, and the whole point.
