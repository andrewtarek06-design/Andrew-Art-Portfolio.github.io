Case study — Art Direction

Sunbulah

Keep it simple.

ClientSunbulah
SectorFMCG / Frozen Food
RoleArt Director
DisciplineArt Direction · Key Visual
01 — The challenge

Make frozen food feel easy, not lazy.

Sunbulah covers everything frozen — veg, strips, sambousa. The visual had to say cooking just got simpler: grab the bag, make the meal, done.

02 — The idea

From bag to plate, effortlessly.

We lead with the finished dish and the ease behind it — no complexity, no fuss, just a good meal.

03 — Craft

Appetite in the details.

Clean styling, warm light and crisp focus keep it fresh, premium and shelf-true.
