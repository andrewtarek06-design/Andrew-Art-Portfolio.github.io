SectorRetail / Mall

01 — The challenge

Escape the back-to-school cliché.

Every brand runs the same back-to-school imagery. City Centre needed to bring back the excitement — and say you'll find everything you need here.

02 — The idea

Turn the routine into a thrill.

School essentials burst into motion and energy, reframing the new term as something to look forward to, not dread.

03 — Craft

Playful, premium, precise.

A bright palette and bold, kinetic composition keep it joyful while staying retail-ready at any size.
