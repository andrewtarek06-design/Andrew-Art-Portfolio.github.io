ClientTom VR

01 — The challenge

Sell a feeling you can't show on a shelf.

At Mall of Egypt you can step into VR and land somewhere that feels completely real. The visual had to capture that leap from a shopping trip into another world.

02 — The idea

Collide the two realities.

One frame, two worlds: mid-shopping, suddenly surrounded by dinosaurs — close enough to feel real.

03 — Craft

Make the impossible believable.

Cinematic scale and dynamic composition sell the immersion, turning a headset into a genuine adventure.
