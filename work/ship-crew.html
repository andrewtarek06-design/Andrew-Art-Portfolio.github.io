Case study — Art Direction

Ship
& Crew

One container to the world.

ClientShip & Crew
SectorLogistics / Shipping
RoleArt Director
DisciplineArt Direction · Key Visual
01 — The challenge

Make logistics feel like possibility.

Ship & Crew moves goods worldwide. The visual had to turn a shipping container into something bigger — a gateway.

02 — The idea

The container as a doorway.

We frame the container as a portal — into Egypt, and out to the whole world — making global reach feel tangible.

03 — Craft

Cinematic and deep.

Moody blues, depth and architectural light give the brand scale and seriousness.
