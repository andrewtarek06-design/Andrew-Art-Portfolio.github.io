Case study — Art Direction

Granite

Save it. Grow it.

ClientGranite
SectorFintech / Investment
RoleArt Director
DisciplineArt Direction · Key Visual
01 — The challenge

Make saving feel like winning.

Granite is an investment app to save and grow your money. The visual had to make personal finance feel aspirational and rewarding, not dry.

02 — The idea

Wealth, within reach.

Cash in motion around a relaxed, confident hero — the feeling of money working for you through the app.

03 — Craft

Bold and premium.

Dramatic lighting and dynamic composition give a finance product real energy and aspiration.
