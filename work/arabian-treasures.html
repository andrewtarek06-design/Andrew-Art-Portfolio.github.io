Case study — Art Direction

Arabian
Treasures

Eastern scents, bottled.

ClientArabian Treasures · EVA Cosmetics
SectorBeauty / Cosmetics
RoleArt Director
DisciplineArt Direction · Key Visual
01 — The challenge

Make a scent you can see.

Arabian Treasures, by EVA Cosmetics, is a body-care line built on Eastern notes — gold, pomegranate, fig and date. The art direction had to make those rich, exotic scents feel visible and desirable.

02 — The idea

Dress beauty in heritage.

We wrap the products in the warmth and opulence of their own ingredients — an indulgent, Arabian sensory world rather than a plain shelf shot.

03 — Craft

Warmth meets luxury.

Rich tones, refined styling and precise light give the line a premium, editorial finish that makes each scent feel like a treasure.
