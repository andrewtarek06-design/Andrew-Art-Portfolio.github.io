Case study — Art Direction

Rotana

Butter that gets you.

ClientRotana
SectorFMCG / Dairy
RoleArt Director
DisciplineArt Direction · Key Visual
01 — The challenge

Give a butter brand warmth and wit.

Rotana's line — butter that understands you — needed a visual that feels homely and knowing, like it gets every recipe you attempt.

02 — The idea

Build it around the cook, not just the product.

A rich, homemade spread frames Rotana as the partner that understands the kitchen — for everyday food and desserts alike.

03 — Craft

Appetite and warmth.

Deep, warm grading and tactile food styling make it feel premium and homemade at once.
