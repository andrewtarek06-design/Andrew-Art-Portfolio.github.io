ClientiVein One
RoleArt Director · AI Artist
DisciplineAI Film · Motion
Runtime0:60
01 — The challenge

Launch a health brand that doesn't feel clinical.

Healthcare defaults to white coats and stock smiles. iVein One needed the opposite — calm, considered, aspirational — a film that feels like a luxury house, on a tight timeline.

02 — The idea

Treat health like a luxury brand.

Marble, tailored suits, unhurried light. The line is simple — your health is your wealth; one place, one visit. AI builds the world and the polish; the art direction keeps it human, warm and premium.

03 — The pipeline

From prompt to picture-lock.

Concept & shot list → AI-generated frames and sequences directed to one consistent luxury look → motion, grade and sound to cut it into a single film. The discipline was keeping every frame on-brand.
